Tokens & foundations

Every visual decision in VetVet traces back to a token on this page. Use semantic names (--color-brand-primary) in product code; use scale names (--color-brand-500) only when building new primitives.

Container & grid

App shell: 224 px sidebar + 1216 px content at 1440 width. Content max-width 1120 px, gutters at 48 px.

  • Section gap between unrelated groups: 48 px
  • Related stacked elements: 16 px
  • Inside a card: 24 px padding
  • Inline, within a control: 8 px
Control heights

Three input sizes cover every screen. Default to md.

  • sm — 28 px · tables, toolbars
  • md — 32 px · default form fields, buttons
  • lg — 40 px · marketing pages, empty states

Border radius

VetVet’s aesthetic is precise, not friendly-rounded. Default radii are small.

Rule of thumb — buttons & inputs use sm; cards & modals use lg; pills & avatars use full. Radius scales with container: a 4 px radius chip inside an 8 px radius card stays visually nested.
